At nearly 59° north, Ridgedale Farm is one of Europe's most influential
small-scale regenerative operations. Richard Perkins combines pastured poultry
and livestock, no-dig market gardening, Keyline design, and agroforestry into a
tightly designed whole — and, notably, turns a profit on a famously short
growing season.

Through his book and courses, Ridgedale has become a template for aspiring
farmers who want a viable living from a small, regenerative holding rather than
scaling up and burning out.
