Las Cañadas is a cooperative Center for Agroecology and Permaculture on more
than 300 hectares in the rare cloud-forest region of Veracruz. It conserves
some 265 hectares of forest and, in 1998, established Mexico's first ecological
easement to protect it in perpetuity.

Around that conserved core, the cooperative practises seed stewardship,
regenerative food production, and education, hosting an annual permaculture
festival — a model of caring for a threatened ecosystem while living from it.
